IN PROGRESS
ARGO Float Conversational AI
Talking to the Ocean
An LLM-powered chatbot that lets anyone — researchers, policy-makers, curious students — query oceanographic data from ARGO floats in plain English. Instead of downloading NetCDF files and writing code, you just ask: "Show me salinity profiles near the equator in March 2023."
Problem: ARGO floats collect critical oceanographic data — temperature, salinity, BGC parameters — but accessing it requires domain expertise and programming skills. The gap between raw data and meaningful insight excludes non-technical decision-makers entirely.
// WHAT I'M BUILDING
  • Ingest ARGO NetCDF files → convert to structured SQL (PostgreSQL) + Parquet format
  • Vector database (FAISS/Chroma) stores metadata and summaries for semantic retrieval
  • RAG pipeline with Model Context Protocol (MCP) — LLM translates natural language → SQL queries
  • Interactive dashboards via Streamlit/Dash: mapped trajectories, depth-time plots, profile comparisons
  • Chatbot interface powered by LLaMA/Mistral/Qwen with query intent understanding
  • PoC with Indian Ocean ARGO data, designed for future extensibility to satellite datasets, BGC floats, gliders
// SAMPLE QUERIES THE SYSTEM HANDLES
  • Show me salinity profiles near the equator in March 2023
  • Compare BGC parameters in the Arabian Sea for the last 6 months
  • What are the nearest ARGO floats to this location?

IN PROGRESS
Hyperspectral Crop Health AI
Precision Agriculture Platform
Agriculture loses billions to delayed pest detection and reactive management. This platform fuses multispectral/hyperspectral imaging with IoT environmental sensor data to shift farmers from reactive to proactive crop management.
Problem: Traditional crop monitoring is delayed and labor-intensive. Soil degradation, pest outbreaks, and unpredictable weather cause significant yield losses that could be prevented with earlier, more precise detection. There's no unified platform that combines spectral imaging with sensor data for field-level AI-driven insights.
// WHAT I'M BUILDING
  • Ingest and align multispectral/hyperspectral image sequences with historical datasets
  • Extract vegetation and soil indices (NDVI, NDRE, etc.) from spectral data
  • LSTM models detect trends in vegetation stress from temporal spectral series
  • CNN models identify disease signatures and pest risk zones from imagery
  • Sensor fusion — soil moisture, air temperature, humidity, leaf wetness contextualize spectral anomalies
  • Zone-specific automated alerts when conditions reach outbreak-conducive thresholds
  • Dashboard: spectral health maps, temporal trend plots, anomaly alerts, risk zone predictions
  • Mobile-friendly notifications + exportable reports for field use
// TARGET USERS
  • Agronomists and agricultural researchers needing precise, field-level data
  • Field technicians who need actionable alerts without complex interpretation
  • Progressive farmers adopting precision agriculture practices
